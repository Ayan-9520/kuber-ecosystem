**Category:** Business (secondary: Finance)

## Console Setup

1. **Create app** → App name: **KuberOne Partner**
2. **App access** → Restricted (partner login required)
3. **Ads** → No
4. **Content rating** → IARC (Business/Finance)
5. **Target audience** → 18+ professionals
6. **Data safety** → Partner data + customer PII handling disclosed
7. **Financial features** → Commission, loan facilitation
